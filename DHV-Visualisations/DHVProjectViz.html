About the chosen dataset: Drug Poisoning in The United States of America

The chosen data contains two layers of data. One is the overall state-wide drug poisoning mortality rate, the other one being the mortality rate for specific racial segments of the United States society (viz. Hispanic, Hispanic Black/White etc).
